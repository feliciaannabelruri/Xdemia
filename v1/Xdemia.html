Building Blocks

Education, at the smallest meaningful level.

A Building Block is a carefully curated set of related learning elements — files, resources, assignments, lab setups and data — that together cover a specific topic.

Teachers update and personalise their materials without rebuilding an entire course from scratch.

Valuable time is saved, while students benefit from content that reflects the latest knowledge, developments, and real-world practice — a modular learning infrastructure that institutions can assemble to fit any programme.

The LMS Bridge

Open education, across every border.

The LMS Bridge lets teachers share their courses and learning content across different LMS systems. Students no longer need to register separately at partner institutions to follow exchange programmes.

It brings the true European spirit of education to life: open, connected, collaborative — and built to move learners forward.
