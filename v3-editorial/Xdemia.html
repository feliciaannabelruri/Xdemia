True modular education

Education, at the smallest meaningful level.

A Building Block is a curated set of related learning elements — files, resources, assignments, lab setups and data — that together cover a specific topic.

Use a whole block, or pull the single element you need straight into your own LMS.

Teachers update and personalise materials without rebuilding a course from scratch — so learners always get content that reflects the latest knowledge and real-world practice.

The LMS Bridge

Open education, across every border.

The LMS Bridge lets teachers share courses and content across different LMS systems. Students no longer register separately at partner institutions to follow exchange programmes.

It brings the true European spirit of education to life: open, connected, collaborative — and built to move learners forward.
